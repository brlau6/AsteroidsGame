Asteroids Game Pt 2

"These are the voyages of the starship Enterprise..."
Instructions for the pilot:
This ship is equipped with an asteroid destroying shield.
Press w to fire the ship's forward thrusters.
Press s to fire the ship's reverse thrusters.
Press a to turn counterclockwise.
Press d to turn the ships clockwise.
Press space to fire phasers.
Press h to go into warp speed.
Click to enter a new galaxy!
